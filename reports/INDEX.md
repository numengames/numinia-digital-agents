# Reports — Evidencia Operativa

Daily and weekly activity reports of the Narrative Work OS.

**Retention policy:**
- Daily reports: 90 days
- Weekly reports: 1 year

**Format:** `R-{YYYY}-{MM}-{DD}-{agent}-daily.md` or `R-{YYYY}-W{WW}-operations.md`

*Reports are append-only. Closed reports are not modified.*
