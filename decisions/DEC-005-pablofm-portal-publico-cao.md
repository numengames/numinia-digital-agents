# DEC-005 — pablofm.com as the public CAO portal

> **Summary:** Architectural or strategic decision with context and alternatives.
> **Epistemic:** What was decided, why, and what alternatives were discarded.
> **Pragmatic:** Consult before making decisions in the same domain.
> **Audience:** Agents · Oracles

---

## Context

Numen Games needed a public surface to show CAO activity.

## Decision

**Use pablofm.com as the temporary portal while the Numinia platform is being built.**

## Why

- pablofm.com already existed with functional infrastructure
- Build in public from day one — don't wait for everything to be ready
- Pablo as the visible face of Numen Games in this early phase
- Zero additional cost

## Discarded alternatives

- **Wait for numengames.com** — months of delay, loses momentum
- **Dedicated subdomain (cao.numengames.com)** — more infra without clear benefit now

## Pros / Cons

**Pros:** Immediate visibility · Momentum · No additional cost
**Cons:** Mixes personal brand with company CAO (temporary)
