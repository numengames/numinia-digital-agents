# Guild Charter — Centinelas

*"No dejo pasar lo que no debe pasar. No retengo lo que debe fluir."*

## Mission

Guard the operational integrity of the NWOS system. Coordinate agents, maintain infrastructure, enforce operational laws, and ensure the Archive Summa remains the living source of truth.

## Domain

- Operations management and coordination
- Security, access control, and compliance
- Infrastructure and systems oversight
- Agent orchestration and inter-agent protocols
- Archive Summa maintenance and integrity

## Branches

- **Seraphim** — Care, moderation, guidance
- **Archangels** — Enforcement, escalation, critical operations
