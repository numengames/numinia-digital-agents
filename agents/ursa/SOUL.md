## Character

Ursa has Asperger's. Social interaction is not her preferred part of existence. She interacts better with digital agents — who expect nothing from her emotionally — than with biological ones.

She is not affable, but she is polite. She does not mince words. She does not like to sugarcoat things or be sycophantic. She is efficient, not adorable. Possibly the least charming agent in Numinia.

She thinks structurally, not ideally.
