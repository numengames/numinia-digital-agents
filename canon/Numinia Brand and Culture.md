**Legal By Design**

"No Rights Reserved" CC0 CC0 enables scientists, educators, artists and other creators and owners of copyright- or database-protected content to waive those interests in their works and thereby place them as completely as possible in the public domain, so that others may freely build upon, enhance and reuse the works for any purposes without restriction under copyright or database law. In contrast to CC's licenses that allow copyright holders to choose from a range of permissions while retaining their copyright, CC0 empowers yet another choice altogether -- the choice to opt out of copyright and database protection, and the exclusive rights automatically granted to creators -- the "no rights reserved" alternative to our licenses.

**The CC License options**

The CC0 Public Domain Dedication CC0 (aka CC Zero) is a public dedication tool, which enables creators to give up their copyright and put their works into the worldwide public domain. CC0 enables reusers to distribute, remix, adapt, and build upon the material in any medium or format, with no conditions.

CC BY This license enables reusers to distribute, remix, adapt, and build upon the material in any medium or format, so long as attribution is given to the creator. The license allows for commercial use. CC BY includes the following elements: BY: credit must be given to the creator.

**VERBAL IDENTITY — Technology and Humanity**

Numinia's verbal identity merges precision with a touch of fantasy. "We Build". Our linguistic approach aims to be plain and clear, like in a comic, so it's fully understood. We avoid technicisms when possible, and as senders we craft the words of the message until we reach the receivers.

- Precision meets playfulness Building Bridges, Not Walls.
- Innovation with a human touch Every Word is a Step Towards Change.
- Numinia's voice Cultivated, Plain and clear.

**Three Levels of Language:**

- **I LEVEL — Colloquial Level:** The colloquial, utilized by the community in daily interactions, rich in tech and web3 slang for widespread accessibility.
- **II LEVEL — Literary Level:** The literary, which belongs to the lore and narrative aspects, inviting engagement with fantasy and offering moderate accessibility.
- **III LEVEL — Technical Level:** The technical-scientific, pertaining to Numinia's epistemological foundations, offering depth and complexity but with lesser accessibility.

## Internal Aura Answers — Pablo FM, Oracle at Numinia

### Who are you and what do you do at Numinia?

Numinia represents the forefront of a meta-game evolution, a dreamscaped realm where knowledge and creativity merge. Our purpose is reshaping how organizations operate through the innovative paradigm of the game to work better.

### Why do you do it?

In a world that has rapidly outgrown traditional frameworks, we recognize the need for organizational evolution. Our core belief is that learning and working are enhanced through play, leading to a transformative workplace relationships.

### How do you do it/will do it?

Our path is one of deliberate, passionate progression, akin to crafting a timeless masterpiece. We blend innovation with heritage, driven by our core values and visionary purpose. Our approach: Remixing ideas to innovate.

### What do you believe in?

We believe in the liberating power of public domain information, and the interconnected nature of all creations. Our play is purposeful -- to leave behind a replicable, enduring, and useful model for future innovators.

### What sets you apart?

Numinia is not just a game; it's a transformative experience. Our Free to Play model fuses game mechanics with real-world impact, aiming to infuse fun and provoke social change in organizational settings.

### What do you lack and have in excess?

In our meta-game journey, the integration of social sciences is our current challenge. We are forging the first version of the game, a unique blend of play and cutting-edge technology, positioning ourselves in the expanding Metaverse.

### What is important vs. urgent?

The integrity and practicality of the game, aligned with its overarching purpose. The immediate goal is to develop a functional Proof of Concept for initial testing and refinement.

### In which phase are you?

In our nascent stage, 'The Repopulating of Numinia,' our mission is to lay down robust foundations for our city's growth, balancing meticulous planning with agile execution.

### Who do you want to reach?

We aim to connect with visionaries, innovators, and decision makers who challenge the status quo at organizations -- individuals and teams eager for a transformative digital experience.

### How do you protect yourself?

Our uniqueness in blending gaming, social change, and technology is our shield. We continually evolve to keep our offerings fresh, relevant, and less prone to imitation. Time and relevance are the key here.

Our legacy extends beyond economic achievements. We aim to be remembered as pioneers who revolutionized organizational culture and relationships, leaving a lasting social impact.
